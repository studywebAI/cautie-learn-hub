Chapter 1: Introduction

The fundamental concept here is that photosynthesis converts light energy into chemical energy. This process occurs in chloroplasts and involves two main stages.

Key points:

  • Light reactions happen in thylakoids
  • Calvin cycle happens in stroma
  • Produces glucose from CO₂ and H₂O
