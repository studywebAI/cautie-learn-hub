STUDYSET - AI STUDENT STUDY PLANNER (5 Complete Workflows)

Concept: Students create personal study sets with a 5-step flow: (1) Name, (2) Material Upload, (3) Select Resources (most important), (4) Plan Days, (5) Daily Overview + Settings. AI generates personalized study plans spread over selected days with integrated agenda.

Key Principle: Name & Description/Subject are BOTH totally optional. Resource selection is the crucial step where students choose what to use (photos, microphone, recent items, text, links, etc.). After generation, ask preference questions (which days most/least learning, triangle distribution). Multiple layout options shown per page.
