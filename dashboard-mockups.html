Component Legend - What Can Go In Each Box?

Select from these component types when customizing your dashboard. Each category shows examples of what data/information can be displayed.

📊 Statistics
Numeric values & metrics Class average, completion rate, student count, grade distribution, performance trends, totals, percentages
📌 Deadlines & Time
Time-based information Due dates, upcoming events, countdown timers, session durations, study time logged, time spent per task
✅ Tasks & Actions
To-do items & activities Assignment list, checklist items, priority levels, submission status, action items, pending reviews
👥 People Data
Student/user information Student roster, attendance records, contributor lists, user profiles, collaboration groups, at-risk students
📚 Learning & Resources
Educational materials Textbook links, video tutorials, study guides, resource lists, course materials, recommended reading
🎮 Gamification
Engagement & motivation XP points, badges earned, level progression, streak counters, achievements, leaderboard rankings, rewards
📈 Analytics & Charts
Data visualization Progress charts, performance graphs, trend lines, distribution histograms, heat maps, comparison charts
💬 Communication
Messaging & alerts Notification counts, alert messages, discussion summaries, feedback snippets, recent announcements, reminders
