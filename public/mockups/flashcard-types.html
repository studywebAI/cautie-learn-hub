Flashcard Type Mockups

Card "types" pick the best-fitting format per card during generation. Front/back always read as short fragments — never as a quiz question — so flashcards stay distinct from the Quiz tool.

Rule
Fragment vs Question
Flashcard sides are never phrased as a question + answer pair — that would just be a quiz. Both sides read as matching fragments: a term/cue on one side, a description/fact on the other.
Wrong — Question Phrasing
Front
What organ pumps blood?
Correct — Fragment Phrasing
Front
the organ that pumps blood

03
Image Card
Only available in Research mode. Front names a concrete, photographable subject; the AI looks up a real photo of it (no invented images) and shows it above the term. Back stays a short description fragment, same as Term & Definition.

Alternates
Suggested Phrasings
The AI auto-fills the best-fitting phrasing per side, but also suggests up to 2 alternative phrasings (same fragment style). Chips under each side let the learner swap to an alternate, or go back to the original — applies to any card type.
Front + Back with Swap Chipsterm-definition
Front
the organ that pumps blood
Front: Original muscular organ circulating blood four-chambered pump in the chest
Back
the heart
Back: Original cardiac muscle organ the human heart
