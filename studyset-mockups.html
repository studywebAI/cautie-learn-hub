Timeline · gamified path

Journey Path

The studyset is a route from today to the exam. Setup asks two questions; everything else is the map. Each day is a stop on the path with its tools; the exam is the finish line. Progress literally moves you down the road — perfect for the "build once, follow day-by-day" promise.

Every topic in your material becomes a card. You move cards across columns — Materials → To study → Studying → Mastered — as you learn. Each card carries its own quick-launch tools. The readiness ring is literally "how many cards reached Mastered."

Side by side

Which direction?

All ten share one design system (the brand green, the same components) so they can be mixed. My recommendation: Concept 1 (Command Center) for create+configure, Concept 5 (Bento Hub) for the detail page, Concept 7 (Calendar-First) for agenda/ICS, and Concept 6 (AI Coach) as an optional onboarding path.

Concept Best for Effort
1 · Command CenterConsistency with Quiz; power without overwhelmMedium
2 · Journey PathMotivation, younger studentsMedium
3 · Study BoardTactile learners, topic masteryHigh
4 · Focus ModeCalm, distraction-freeLow
5 · Bento HubGlanceable detail page · premium feelMedium
6 · AI CoachZero-friction onboardingMedium
7 · Calendar-FirstICS sync · agenda integrationLow*
8 · PocketMobile / on-the-goHigh
9 · Analytics ProData-driven studentsMedium
10 · Class HubSchools · sharing · teachersHigh

* much of the calendar/ICS plumbing already exists in your codebase.
